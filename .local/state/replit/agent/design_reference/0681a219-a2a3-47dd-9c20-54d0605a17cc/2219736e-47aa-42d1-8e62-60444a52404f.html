Problem & Solution

Problem

Personal trainers are expensive and scheduling is difficult for busy professionals who want to maintain their fitness but struggle with consistency and proper form.

Solution

An AI-powered mobile app that creates personalized workout plans, provides real-time form correction through computer vision, and adapts to user progress and preferences.

Market & Business Model

Target Market

Primary market: Busy professionals aged 25-45 with disposable income. Secondary market: Fitness enthusiasts looking for convenience and personalization.

Business Model

Freemium model with basic workouts free, premium subscription at $9.99/month for personalized plans and form analysis, and corporate packages for workplace wellness.

Team & Competition

Team

Founder with 5 years of fitness industry experience, technical co-founder with ML background, advisor with previous startup exits in health tech.

Competition Analysis

Direct competitors include Fitbod and Nike Training Club. Our competitive advantage is real-time form correction using computer vision and more personalized AI recommendations.

AI Feedback

Strong Problem-Solution Fit

The problem is clearly defined and affects a large market segment.

Consider Technical Complexity

Real-time form correction requires significant AI expertise and computational resources.

Recommendation

Start with simpler features and gradually add AI capabilities as you grow.

Suggested Next Steps

Conduct user interviews with target market
Build MVP with basic workout tracking
Research computer vision technology partners
Validate pricing with potential customers
