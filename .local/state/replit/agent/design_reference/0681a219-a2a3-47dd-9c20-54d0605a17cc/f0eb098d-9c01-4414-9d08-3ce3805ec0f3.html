AI-Powered Fitness Coach

High viability score - Strong market potential

Problem & Solution

Problem

Personal fitness coaching is expensive and inaccessible to most people. Many struggle with consistency, proper form, and personalized workout plans that adapt to their progress and limitations.

Solution

An AI-powered mobile app that provides personalized fitness coaching using computer vision to analyze form, adaptive workout plans based on user progress, and real-time feedback. The app includes nutrition guidance, progress tracking, and community features.

Target Market

Health-conscious individuals aged 25-45, primarily urban professionals who want fitness guidance but lack time or budget for personal trainers.

Market Size: Large (100M+ people)
Geographic: Global

Business Model

Freemium subscription model with basic workouts free and premium features including AI form analysis, personalized nutrition plans, and advanced analytics at $9.99/month.

Revenue: Subscription
Price: $9.99/month

Competition

Main competitors include Nike Training Club, MyFitnessPal, and Freeletics. Our differentiator is the AI-powered form analysis using computer vision, which most competitors lack.

Medium Competition

Team

Founding team of 3: ML engineer with computer vision experience, former personal trainer, and mobile app developer. Combined 15+ years experience in fitness and technology.
